About Sam Fisher

Sam Fisher is a highly decorated NAVY Seal and former CIA paramilitary ops/clandestine officer. Also, he is a former employee of Third Echelon, a top-secret black bag operation sub-branch within the National Security Agency (NSA) and a former member of its subsequent "Splinter Cell" program.

Sam Fisher is known as the "ultimate predator".
